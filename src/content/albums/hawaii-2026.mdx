intro: This was our second trip to both Oahu and Maui, and the first time Cait's DSLR had seen the light of day since highschool. Cait had a run-in with food poisoning on the first day, so I got to spend some time with the farm dog Bingo while she recovered. Once she was back on her feet, we explored a lot of the North Shore, hitting up some of our favorite spots from last time and discovering new ones. Maui was a bit more relaxed, with plenty of time spent swimming with turtles and watching whales breach on the horizon. Overall, a fantastic trip filled with great food, better drinks, beautiful scenery, and lots of memories.
